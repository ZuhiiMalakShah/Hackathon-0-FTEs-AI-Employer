## Core Product

**TechCorp Suite** — An all-in-one project management and team collaboration platform designed for software development teams, marketing agencies, and professional services firms.

### Product Lines

| Product | Description | Target Audience |
|---------|-------------|-----------------|
| **TechCorp Projects** | Kanban boards, sprints, Gantt charts, time tracking | Engineering & product teams |
| **TechCorp Docs** | Collaborative documentation with version history | All teams |
| **TechCorp Connect** | Team messaging, video calls, screen sharing | All teams |
| **TechCorp Analytics** | Project dashboards, velocity charts, burndown reports | Managers & leads |
